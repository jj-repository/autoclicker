# AutoClicker

Python/PyQt6 automated mouse clicker and keyboard presser with dual clicker support. Two backends: pynput (cross-platform, PyQt6 GUI) and evdev (Linux Wayland/games, tkinter GUI). Stack: Python, PyQt6, pynput.
